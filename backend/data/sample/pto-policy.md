# Paid Time Off Policy

## Accrual

Full-time employees accrue PTO at **1.5 days per month** (18 days per
calendar year), credited on the 1st of each month. Accrual starts on the
hire-month 1st if the start date is on or before the 15th; otherwise it
starts the following month. Part-time employees (under 30 hours/week) accrue
at 0.75 days per month. Interns and contractors do not accrue PTO; they use
unpaid time off coordinated with their manager in HubNorth.

There is no separate floating-holiday bank. Company holidays are unpaid for
hourly contractors and paid for employees under the holiday schedule below.

## How to request

Submit requests in HubNorth → Time Off → Request, at least **5 business days**
before the first day off for absences of three days or fewer, and **15
business days** for absences of four days or more. Same-day sick leave is an
exception — enter it in HubNorth within 24 hours and notify your manager on
Slack. Do not email People Ops for routine requests; HubNorth is the system
of record.

Include coverage notes (who owns on-call, who watches `#deploys`) for any
role that participates in the weekly on-call rotation.

## Approval

Direct managers approve or deny in HubNorth within three business days.
Requests spanning more than ten consecutive workdays also need a director
approval (Priya Natarajan for Engineering, Lucas Berg for Customer Success,
Mira Okonkwo for People & Ops). People Ops does not second-guess day-to-day
approvals unless HubNorth flags a negative balance or a holiday blackout.

Blackout windows: the last full week of each fiscal quarter for Field Ops
and Customer Success, listed annually in HubNorth under Time Off → Blackouts.

## Company holidays (US)

Observed: New Year's Day, Memorial Day, Independence Day, Labor Day,
Thanksgiving Day, day after Thanksgiving, Christmas Eve, Christmas Day. If a
holiday falls on Saturday, the preceding Friday is observed; if Sunday, the
following Monday. Boulder and Austin offices close on these days. Eindhoven
follows the Dutch public holiday calendar published each January in HubNorth.

## Carryover

Unused PTO carries over up to a **cap of 10 days**. Balance above the cap on
December 31 is forfeited. There is no cash-out of unused PTO except at
termination where local law requires it. Mid-year transfers between US and
Eindhoven retain accrued days; the local holiday calendar switches on the
transfer effective date.

## Sick leave

Sick leave is drawn from the same PTO balance. After three consecutive sick
days, a manager may ask for a doctor's note uploaded to HubNorth. Extended
medical leave beyond accrued PTO follows the separate Leave of Absence
process (HubNorth → Leave); contact Mira Okonkwo. This policy does not cover
short-term disability benefit amounts — those live with the benefits carrier
portal linked from HubNorth, not in this wiki.
