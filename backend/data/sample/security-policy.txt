Northwind Robotics Security Policy (employee summary). This note is plain text for the internal wiki mirror. It does not replace the full policy PDF attached in HubNorth under Security → Policies.

Passwords and MFA. All Northwind accounts use SSO through HubNorth identity. Passwords must be at least 14 characters and are stored only in 1Password Business — do not keep production credentials in browsers, notes apps, or Slack. MFA is mandatory for HubNorth, GitHub, Fleet OS Console, and NorthVPN. Preferred second factor is a hardware security key; TOTP apps are allowed for contractors. SMS MFA is disabled. If you lose your security key, open a ForgeDesk ticket under Access Recovery and bring photo ID to the Boulder or Austin office, or schedule a video verify with IT for remote staff.

NorthVPN for production. NorthVPN is required for any access to staging or production clusters, Fleet OS Console, and the internal package mirror. Split tunnel is off by default — do not change that setting. Connecting from public Wi-Fi is allowed only while NorthVPN is up. Personal devices may not install the NorthVPN profile. Contractors use short-lived profiles that expire at contract end.

Data classification. Treat customer warehouse layouts, fleet telemetry, and badge access logs as Confidential. Internal wiki pages and non-customer metrics are Internal. Public marketing site copy is Public. Do not upload Confidential data to personal Drive accounts or unapproved SaaS. When in doubt, ask security@northwind-robotics.example before sharing externally.

Laptop encryption. Company laptops must keep FileVault (macOS) or BitLocker (Windows) enabled. Disk encryption is checked by northwind doctor during onboarding and again at the monthly IT compliance scan. Unencrypted devices are blocked from NorthVPN until remediated. Report a lost or stolen laptop to security@northwind-robotics.example within one hour; IT will wipe the device via MDM.

Reporting a security incident. Phishing, suspected account takeover, malware alerts, or accidental Confidential data exposure must be reported immediately to security@northwind-robotics.example and by opening a PagerDuty incident on service "security-intake" if customer data may be involved. Do not forward suspicious mail to coworkers for "is this real" checks — forward only to security. Priya Natarajan escalates Sev-1 security events to Marcus Chen within two hours. Retaliation for good-faith reports is prohibited under the Code of Conduct course in HubNorth Learning.
