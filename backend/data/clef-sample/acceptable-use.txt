Acceptable Use of Company Devices

Company devices are provided primarily for business purposes.

Limited personal use is fine as long as it:
- Does not interfere with your work
- Does not violate company policy (see Employee Privacy for what to expect about monitoring)
- Does not consume excessive network resources

The following are prohibited:
- Installing software outside the software request process
- Illegal downloads
- Sharing credentials
- Circumventing security controls
- Connecting unknown USB devices

Repeated violations will be addressed by the founders and may result in disciplinary action.
