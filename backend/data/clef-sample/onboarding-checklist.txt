New Hire Checklist

Before your first day (handled by us):
- Signed offer letter and proprietary information agreement in your Personnel folder
- Google Apps, Slack, and Trello accounts created
- Two-factor authentication ready to set up with you
- Desk set up with your equipment

Your first day (starts at 10:00):
- Set up your accounts and change your temporary password
- Set up two-factor authentication
- Join the team Slack channels
- Push something public live (a commit, a design change, or a blog post)
- Team lunch

Your first weeks:
- Review the employee handbook
- Weekly 1:1 with the founder you report to
- Background reading from the onboarding documents
