Clef Password Policy

All employees must use unique passwords for company systems.

Password requirements:
- Minimum length: 14 characters
- At least one uppercase letter
- At least one lowercase letter
- At least one number
- At least one special character

Passwords may not contain:
- Employee names
- The company name
- Birthdays
- Common dictionary words by themselves

Two-factor authentication is required for:
- Email (Google Apps)
- VPN
- Git hosting
- Payroll
- Administrative dashboards

Passwords must never be shared with anyone or sent through email, Slack, or Trello.

If you suspect a password has been compromised, change it immediately and tell the founders.

Service account passwords are rotated every 90 days.
